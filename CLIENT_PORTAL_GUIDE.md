## Welcome to AFYA

Welcome to AFYA - your personalized wellness platform! We're here to help you achieve your health and fitness goals with science-backed programs, personalized guidance, and supportive tools.

### What is AFYA?

AFYA is a wellness-tech platform that makes elite-level fitness, nutrition, and health education accessible to everyone. We believe in:
- **Community**: Supporting each other on our wellness journeys
- **Discipline**: Consistent, sustainable progress
- **Joy**: Finding happiness in movement and healthy living

### What You'll Find in Your Portal

- **Personalized Assessments**: Tell us about your goals and needs
- **Custom Wellness Packets**: Receive tailored programs and guidance
- **Health Tools**: Access calculators and trackers
- **Progress Tracking**: Monitor your wellness journey
- **Resources**: Educational content and support

## Getting Started

### Setting Up Your Account

1. **Check Your Email**
   - Look for an email from AFYA with subject "Welcome to AFYA - Set Up Your Account"
   - Click the setup link (valid for 7 days)

2. **Create Your Password**
   - Choose a strong password (at least 8 characters)
   - Include uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
   - Confirm your password

3. **Complete Your Profile**
   - Verify your name and email
   - Add any additional information requested
   - Click "Complete Setup"

4. **Log In**
   - Navigate to theafya.org
   - Click "Login" in the top navigation
   - Enter your email and password
   - Click "Sign In"

### First Time Login

After your first login, you'll see:
- A welcome message
- Quick tour of the portal (optional)
- Recommended next steps

## Completing Assessments

Assessments help us understand your unique needs and create personalized wellness packets for you.

### Available Assessments

**Nutrition Assessment**
- Dietary preferences and restrictions
- Eating habits and goals
- Nutritional knowledge level

**Training Assessment**
- Exercise experience and history
- Current fitness level
- Training preferences

**Performance Assessment** (for athletes)
- Sport-specific information
- Performance goals
- Training schedule

**Youth Assessment** (for young athletes)
- Age-appropriate questions
- Growth and development considerations
- Activity preferences

**Recovery Assessment**
- Injury history
- Current limitations
- Recovery goals

**Lifestyle Assessment**
- Sleep patterns
- Stress levels
- Daily habits
- Work-life balance

### How to Complete an Assessment

1. **Start an Assessment**
   - Go to "Assessments" in the menu
   - Choose the assessment type
   - Click "Start Assessment"

2. **Answer Questions**
   - Read each question carefully
   - Provide honest, accurate answers
   - Use "Save Progress" to continue later
   - All information is confidential

3. **Review Your Answers**
   - Check your responses before submitting
   - Edit any answers if needed
   - Click "Submit Assessment"

4. **Wait for Your Packet**
   - Your wellness packet will be generated
   - You'll receive a notification when ready
   - Usually available within 24-48 hours

### Tips for Assessments

✅ **Do**:
- Be honest about your current fitness level
- Include all relevant health information
- Mention any injuries or limitations
- Take your time - accuracy matters
- Save progress if you need a break

❌ **Don't**:
- Rush through questions
- Skip important information
- Exaggerate your abilities
- Forget to mention injuries

## Viewing Your Wellness Packets

Your wellness packets are personalized programs created just for you based on your assessment responses.

### What's in a Wellness Packet?

Depending on your assessment type, packets may include:

**Exercise Programs**
- Customized workout plans
- Exercise descriptions and cues
- Sets, reps, and rest periods
- Progression guidelines
- Video demonstrations (when available)

**Nutrition Guidance**
- Meal planning templates
- Portion recommendations
- Healthy eating guidelines
- Recipe ideas
- Hydration goals

**Educational Content**
- Wellness principles
- Form and technique tips
- Recovery strategies
- Goal-setting guidance

**Progress Tracking**
- Metrics to monitor
- Milestone markers
- Reflection prompts

### Accessing Your Packets

1. **View Packets**
   - Click "Packets" in the menu
   - See all your available packets
   - Packets are organized by type and date

2. **Download PDF**
   - Click on a packet to view details
   - Click "Download PDF" button
   - Save to your device
   - Print if desired

3. **Review Content**
   - Read through your entire packet
   - Watch any linked videos
   - Note any questions for your coach
   - Start implementing the program

### Using Your Packets

**Getting Started**
1. Read your packet thoroughly
2. Gather any needed equipment
3. Schedule your workouts
4. Prepare your nutrition plan
5. Set up tracking system

**Following the Program**
- Stick to the prescribed exercises and nutrition
- Track your progress
- Note how you feel
- Adjust as needed (with coach guidance)
- Celebrate small wins

**Questions or Concerns**
- Contact your coach through the platform
- Don't modify the program without guidance
- Report any pain or discomfort
- Ask for clarifications

## Using Health Tools

AFYA provides free health calculators to support your wellness journey.

### Available Tools

**BMR/TDEE Energy Calculator**
- Calculate your basal metabolic rate
- Determine daily calorie needs
- Adjust for activity level
- Plan nutrition accordingly

**Plate Builder**
- Visualize balanced meals
- Portion control guidance
- Macronutrient distribution
- Meal planning support

**Heart Rate Zones Calculator**
- Calculate training heart rate zones
- Optimize workout intensity
- Track cardiovascular fitness
- Improve training efficiency

**Hydration & Sleep Snapshot**
- Assess hydration needs
- Track sleep quality
- Get personalized recommendations
- Improve recovery

**Youth Corner** (for young athletes)
- Age-appropriate health information
- Growth and development tracking
- Youth-specific guidance

**Recovery Check-in**
- Assess recovery status
- Identify areas needing attention
- Optimize rest and recovery
- Prevent overtraining

### How to Use Tools

1. **Access Tools**
   - Click "Tools" in the menu
   - Browse available calculators
   - Select the tool you want to use

2. **Enter Information**
   - Fill in required fields
   - Provide accurate measurements
   - Use the units specified
   - Click "Calculate"

3. **View Results**
   - Review your personalized results
   - Read the explanations
   - Note recommendations
   - Save results (if logged in)

4. **Save for Later**
   - Click "Save Results"
   - Access from "Saved Tools"
   - Track changes over time
   - Compare previous results

### Tips for Using Tools

- Use tools regularly to track progress
- Save results to see trends
- Combine multiple tools for comprehensive insights
- Share results with your coach if helpful
- Re-calculate as your fitness changes

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Account & Access

**Q: I didn't receive my setup email. What should I do?**
A: Check your spam folder. If still not found, contact support@theafya.org

**Q: My setup link expired. How do I get a new one?**
A: Contact support@theafya.org and we'll send a new link.

**Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions.

**Q: Can I access AFYA on my phone?**
A: Yes! The portal is fully mobile-responsive and works on all devices.

### Assessments

**Q: How long do assessments take?**
A: Most assessments take 10-20 minutes. You can save progress and return later.

**Q: Can I change my assessment answers?**
A: Contact your coach if you need to update information after submission.

**Q: Do I need to complete all assessments?**
A: Only complete assessments relevant to your goals. Your coach will guide you.

**Q: How often should I retake assessments?**
A: Typically every 3-6 months, or when your goals or circumstances change significantly.

### Wellness Packets

**Q: When will I receive my packet?**
A: Packets are usually ready within 24-48 hours after assessment completion.

**Q: Can I request changes to my packet?**
A: Yes! Contact your coach to discuss modifications.

**Q: What if I don't understand something in my packet?**
A: Reach out to your coach for clarification. We're here to help!

**Q: Can I share my packet with others?**
A: Your packet is personalized for you. Others should complete their own assessments.

### Health Tools

**Q: Do I need an account to use health tools?**
A: No, tools are free for everyone. An account lets you save results.

**Q: How accurate are the calculators?**
A: Tools use evidence-based formulas but provide estimates. Consult healthcare providers for medical advice.

**Q: Can I use tools on mobile?**
A: Yes! All tools are mobile-friendly.

### Technical Issues

**Q: The website isn't loading properly. What should I do?**
A: Try refreshing the page, clearing your browser cache, or using a different browser.

**Q: I can't download my packet PDF. Help!**
A: Ensure pop-ups are enabled for theafya.org. Contact support if issues persist.

**Q: Videos aren't playing. What's wrong?**
A: Check your internet connection and ensure your browser is up to date.
